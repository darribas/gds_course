Do-It-Yourself¶

Warning

ToDo:

Practice your data tidying skills with a different dataset. For example, you can have a look at the Guardian’s version of Wikileaks’ Afghanistan war logs. The table is stored on a GoogleDoc on the following address:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1EAx8_ksSCmoWW_SlhFyq2QrRn0FNNhcg1TtDFJzZRgc/edit?hl=en#gid=1

Follow these steps:

  • Download the table as a csv file (File –> Download as –> .csv, current sheet).

  • Read it into Python.

  • Explore it by creating a few plots.

  • Examine its level of tidiness and turn it into a fully tidy dataset.

  • Obtain a monthly total count of casualties and create a line or a bar plot of them.
